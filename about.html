OUR STORY

Jaysee Electrical and Industrial Supplies Trading was founded from an idea shaped by years of experience in the shipping industry. Ray Anthony Sia, a former purchaser for a shipping company, developed deep knowledge of the various supplies needed for ships and maritime operations. He realized that many of these items—such as electrical wires, industrial hoses, and electrical equipment—were also widely needed by individuals and other industries. Since most suppliers offered only specific products, buyers often had to visit multiple stores to complete their purchases. To solve this challenge, he established Jaysee Electrical and Industrial Supplies Trading as a one-stop shop where customers can conveniently find a wide range of materials and equipment in one place.

ORDER & TRANSACTION PROCESS

At present, Jaysee Electrical and Industrial Supplies Trading operates without a physical store or warehouse, with its office currently based at a residence in Balagtas, Bulacan. As a growing business, orders are handled directly through email and other contact methods instead of online shopping platforms. Once a client places an order, the requested items are arranged and delivered promptly— similar to a direct order-and-deliver setup that ensures customers receive their supplies efficiently.
